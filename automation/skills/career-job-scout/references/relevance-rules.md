# Relevance rules

Judge the responsibilities as well as the title. Include roles centered on developing, producing, testing, or iterating advertising creative for growth, paid social, direct-response, ecommerce, or UGC programs.

## Strong matches

- Creative Strategist, Senior Creative Strategist, or Lead Creative Strategist
- Performance Creative Strategist
- Paid Social Creative Strategist
- UGC Strategist or UGC Creative Strategist
- Growth Creative Strategist

## Include when responsibilities fit

- Performance Creative or Growth Creative
- Creative Producer or Performance Creative Producer
- UGC Creator, Content Creator, or Creator Strategist
- Video Editor, Paid Social Editor, or Performance Editor
- Creative Lead, Content Strategist, or Social Creative Strategist
- Creative Director focused substantially on performance or direct-response work

Require evidence such as ad concepting, briefs, hooks, scripts, paid-social iteration, performance analysis, creator direction, UGC development, or conversion-focused editing.

## Exclude by default

- Editorial, newsroom, publishing, copy desk, or book editor roles
- Product, business, brand-only, UX, content-design, or corporate strategy roles without advertising-creative ownership
- Film/television post-production roles unrelated to marketing performance
- General graphic design, social community management, account management, or media buying without creative responsibility
- Engineering roles involving editing software or content systems
- Internships unless their work is clearly within the strong-match scope

## Confidence

- **High:** Title and responsibilities both fit. Create a draft.
- **Medium:** Title is adjacent but responsibilities substantially fit. Create a draft and explain the match in the scan report.
- **Low:** Only a keyword fits or the description is unavailable. Do not create a draft; list it as ambiguous.
