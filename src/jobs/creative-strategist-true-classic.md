True Classic is hiring a Creative Strategist to develop persona-driven creative strategy and performance-informed messaging across paid media channels.

## What you’ll do

- Analyze creative performance across hooks, messaging, structure, tone, offers, and claims to shape testing roadmaps.
- Turn customer insights, customer experience feedback, and media performance data into scalable creative systems and campaign concepts.
- Build persona-based messaging frameworks and write direct-response hooks, scripts, captions, headlines, and landing-page concepts.
- Author creative briefs with clear hypotheses, strategic insights, success metrics, and execution guidance.
- Develop platform-native strategies for Meta, TikTok, YouTube and Shorts, and connected TV.
- Partner with Creative, Media Buying, Analytics, Customer Insights, production, legal, and brand teams.
- Use AI tools to accelerate research, ideation, copy development, and creative workflows.

## What you bring

- Experience in creative strategy, performance marketing, copywriting, or direct-response advertising.
- Familiarity with Meta, TikTok, YouTube and Shorts, connected TV, ad managers, Looker, and creative analytics tools.
- Strong strategic thinking, copywriting, creative analysis, and consumer psychology skills.
- The ability to translate performance data into testable creative concepts and measurable business outcomes.
- Strong cross-functional collaboration skills and comfort managing fast-paced creative workflows.
- Familiarity with direct-response frameworks and lightweight prototyping tools such as CapCut, Premiere, Figma, Photoshop, or Canva is a plus.

This hybrid role is based in Calabasas and requires three days per week in the office. Candidates must live within 45 miles of the area.
