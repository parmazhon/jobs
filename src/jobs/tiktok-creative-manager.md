Dr. Squatch is hiring a TikTok Creative Manager to own the brand’s TikTok strategy, lead creative direction, grow the channel, and manage external creators and agency partners.

## What you’ll do

- Develop TikTok content plans around campaigns, product launches, audience growth, and brand relevance.
- Source and guide creators, manage TikTok Live agencies and creative partners, and provide clear briefs and feedback.
- Develop concepts, moodboards, and scalable content formats while maintaining the Dr. Squatch brand voice.
- Create and publish in-feed video, including appearing on camera when appropriate.
- Analyze content and livestream performance, test new formats, and turn insights into creative improvements.
- Partner with social, marketing, and growth teams on broader campaigns and goals.

## What you bring

- At least 4 years of experience creating and directing social content, with a strong emphasis on TikTok.
- At least 4 years of experience working with creators, agencies, or other creative collaborators.
- Strong knowledge of TikTok culture, trends, analytics, and performance metrics.
- Hands-on editing and production skills using tools such as CapCut, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, or TikTok’s native editor.
- Confidence performing on camera and representing a brand voice authentically.
- Strong creative storytelling, communication, and community-building skills.

This hybrid role is based at Dr. Squatch’s Marina del Rey headquarters and requires three days per week in the office.
