Glossier is hiring a contract Content Creator to develop timely, social-first photo and video content across TikTok, Instagram, Reels, Stories, and emerging platforms.

## What you’ll do

- Concept, capture, and edit reactive, trend-driven content using an iPhone and lightweight creator setups.
- Support social calendars, launches, campaigns, events, shoots, PR moments, and community activations.
- Identify emerging creators, formats, sounds, memes, and other shifts in internet culture.
- Participate in UGC-style and creator-led content while keeping the work distinctly Glossier.
- Partner with Brand Marketing, Creative, PR, Influencer, Retail, and Product Marketing teams.

## What you bring

- At least 1–3 years of experience creating social-first content for a brand, agency, publication, or personal platform.
- Strong knowledge of TikTok, Instagram, and creator and influencer ecosystems.
- Independent short-form shooting and editing experience using tools such as CapCut, InShot, TikTok editing tools, or Adobe Premiere.
- Strong visual storytelling instincts across beauty, lifestyle, and culture.
- A portfolio of social or brand content and flexibility around events, shoots, launches, and cultural moments.

This is an initial three-month contract for 30–40 hours per week. The role follows a hybrid schedule with two to three days per week at Glossier’s Soho headquarters.
